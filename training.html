Training, Workouts, & Group Sessions

Training Sessions

The Athlete's Den offers training sessions for softball, baseball, and basketball with experienced coaches who will be able to analayze your movements to determine any areas in need of improvement. Their expert knowledge and ability to examine your swing, throw, or shooting mechanics will help you develop better form and increase your knowledge in the game.

Workouts

Each workout is tailored for every athlete and their sport. A coach will determine your stengths and weaknesses, then develop specific workout routines each week to help you become the best version of yourself!

Group Sessions

A less expensive alternative to the one-on-one training sessions is the group session. The group sessions are extremely beneficial to growing in your sport and increasing your sportmanship! In these sessions, you will undergo coaching while interacting with other athletes; you may perform the same drills or work together using your individual skills to complete tasks.

A variety of training session and workout packages are available for whatever fits your needs best! The Den would be happy to build a custom package just for you!
