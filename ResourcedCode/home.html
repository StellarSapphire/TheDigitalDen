About the Den!

The Athlete's Den is a training facility for all athletes and provides a location for various sports to practice and train. The training facility will be primarily equipped for softball and basketball practices, workouts, and coaching.

  • The Athlete’s Den seeks to create a hard-working community among athletes of all levels and ages.
  • The Den offers one-on-one lessons with experienced coaches
  • Group workouts are available at a discounted price
  • Informational workshops on topics like sports nutrition, injury prevention, and mental resilience are offered.
